About Us

Trips And Tours Are An Expression Of Identity.

Every trip to a new or familiar locations are about more than just sightseeing. At easyGo, we believe that each trip is a way for you to express your African identity. Where you go cannot separate you from who you are

why work with easygo?

We Want To See your Business Grow.

We know that it's a lot of work to run a tourism focused company. That's why easyGo exists; to streamline your operations. We provide a suite of services that make it easy to collect payment, communicate with your customers and track bookings.

The tasks of a modern tourism focused company are more than just planning trips. This can take your focus away from the core of your business; creating memorable experiences. That is why we provide Value Added Services, so that you can stay true to your company vision.
